CAPABILITIES

What makes Kling 3 a production-facing option.

Multimodal instruction handling

Kling AI's public 3.0 materials describe deeper multimodal instruction parsing and cross-task fusion. In practice, write prompts that combine subject, action, camera, lighting, sound, and scene logic.

Scene and shot continuity

Use it when you need the clip to feel like a designed shot rather than a loose animation: consistent subject, clear movement, and a readable visual arc.

Lighting and sound direction

Kling 3 is positioned around visual and audio narrative control. Even if your final export settings vary, include mood, ambience, music cues, or sound design in the brief.

Longer-form planning

For complex ideas, break the concept into shot beats and generate proof clips before assembling a longer sequence or campaign package.

WHEN TO USE IT

Choose Kling 3 when the clip needs a stronger creative spine.

Polished brand video

Good for hero shots, cinematic product scenes, and visual directions that need stakeholder-ready polish.

Complex scene briefs

Use it when your prompt includes multiple constraints: character, environment, camera move, lighting, rhythm, and final frame.

PROMPT STARTERS

Give Kling 3 a shot plan.

Cinematic hero shot

4K cinematic product film, [product] emerges through soft mist, controlled crane movement, dramatic rim light, subtle mechanical sound, premium launch-film mood.

Character scene

A realistic scene of [character] entering [environment], consistent face and outfit, warm practical lighting, slow tracking shot, ambient city sound.

Storyboard beat

Shot 1 establishes the location, shot 2 follows the subject, shot 3 lands on a clean final frame with room for headline text.

Start with Kling O3 Mini for fast exploration. Move to Kling 3 when you know the direction and need more detailed scene control.
