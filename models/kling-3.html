PROMPT STARTER

Create a 12-second multi-shot product story for [product]. Shot 1: wide establishing scene with brand color palette. Shot 2: smooth tracking shot as the product enters frame. Shot 3: close-up detail with consistent logo and lighting. Add subtle native ambience and hold the final frame for CTA text.

HOW TO USE IT

Turn the model into a campaign asset workflow.

  1. Start with the job. Define the exact asset you need: ad concept, launch video, product image, social post, caption set, or campaign brief.
  2. Add production constraints. Include audience, channel, format, brand tone, visual references, and what must stay consistent.
  3. Generate a proof. Review the first output for clarity, consistency, text readability, motion, or factual accuracy before scaling.
  4. Build the asset pack. Use the winning direction to create supporting visuals, captions, scripts, music direction, and ad variants in ChatArt Pro.

PRODUCTION RECIPE

Polished scene production recipe.

Use Kling 3 after the campaign direction is clearer and the team needs a more presentation-ready motion scene. Prepare a short shot list, describe camera movement, lighting, subject behavior, and final frame, then generate the hero clip. Pair it with key visual, headline, and landing-page angle assets so stakeholders can review the full creative direction.
