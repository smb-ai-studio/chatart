CAPABILITIES

What Seedance 2 is especially useful for.

Multimodal references

Public Seedance 2 materials describe a unified audio-video generation architecture that can work from text, image, audio, and video inputs. In ChatArt, that makes it a strong choice when your brief already includes references, beats, or a visual board.

Motion stability

Use it when camera movement, subject continuity, and scene rhythm matter more than producing a one-off visual experiment.

Director-style control

Seedance 2 is positioned around performance, lighting, shadow, and camera-movement control. Write prompts like a short shot list: subject, action, lens, movement, lighting, mood, and ending frame.

Audio-video thinking

If your concept depends on sound, pacing, or musical timing, start here and keep the audio direction close to the video brief from the beginning.
