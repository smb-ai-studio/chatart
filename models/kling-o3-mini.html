PROMPT STARTER

A practical prompt to adapt in ChatArt Pro.

Copy-paste prompt

Create a fast 4K social video draft for [product or topic]. Start with a strong first-second hook, show one clear product or idea moment, use smooth handheld motion, natural lighting, and end with a clean frame for caption text. Generate a version that feels native to short-form feeds.

HOW TO USE IT

Turn the model into a practical prompt workflow.

  1. Start with the job. Define the exact asset you need: ad concept, launch video, product image, social post, caption set, or campaign brief.
  2. Add production constraints. Include audience, channel, format, brand tone, visual references, and what must stay consistent.
  3. Generate a proof. Review the first output for clarity, consistency, text readability, motion, or factual accuracy before scaling.
  4. Build a practice set. Use the strongest direction to practice supporting visuals, captions, scripts, music direction, and creative variants in ChatArt Pro.

PRODUCTION RECIPE

Fast 4K draft production recipe.

Use Kling O3 Mini when the team needs several fast video directions before choosing a final route. Generate three short drafts from one brief: product reveal, creator hook, and lifestyle scene. Keep the draft that has the clearest opening frame, then turn it into a refined script, thumbnail, caption set, and ad variant in ChatArt Pro.
