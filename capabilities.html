CAPABILITY WORKFLOW

Use capabilities in sequence, not as separate tools.

The highest-value ChatArt Pro workflow connects models and asset types around one campaign brief.

  1. 01

    Plan with a writing model

    Use a chat route to turn the brief into audience insight, concept angles, script ideas, captions, and review criteria.

  2. 02

    Explore visuals with image routes

    Generate key visuals, product treatments, thumbnails, moodboards, and ad image variants before committing to video.

  3. 03

    Produce motion with video routes

    Turn the strongest visual direction into product clips, creator scenes, launch videos, or social ad drafts.

  4. 04

    Package the final asset set

    Add music direction, captions, landing-page angles, thumbnail text, and ad variants so the idea can move into review or testing.

PROMPT PATTERNS

Use different prompt structures for different asset jobs.

Video asset prompt

Create a [duration] video for [product/campaign]. Audience: [audience]. Channel: [platform]. Opening hook: [hook]. Visual style: [style]. Camera movement: [movement]. Final frame should leave space for [headline/CTA].

Image asset prompt

Create a campaign key visual for [product] with [mood], [background], [lighting], and [composition]. Leave clean space for headline text. Avoid unreadable text, distorted logos, or confusing product details.

Copy asset prompt

Write five caption hooks, three ad headlines, and two CTA options for [audience] promoting [offer]. Keep the tone [brand tone], avoid hype, and connect each copy option to the same visual idea.
