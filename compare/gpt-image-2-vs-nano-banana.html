GPT Image 2 vs Nano Banana

Use Nano Banana when you want range and surprise. Use GPT Image 2 when the direction is chosen and the image needs stronger prompt following, editing control, or campaign-ready polish.

HOW TO CHOOSE

A practical image workflow.

Start wide with Nano Banana

Ask for five or more visual territories. Look for the idea that has the best composition, mood, and brand fit.

Polish with GPT Image 2

Take the winning direction into a more specific prompt. Add layout requirements, product details, typography needs, and final channel constraints.

TEST PROMPT

Use one brief, then compare outputs differently.

Try: "Create a campaign key visual for [product], premium but friendly, clean composition, soft gradient background, space for a short headline, suitable for social and landing-page use."Judge Nano Banana by variety and fresh directions. Judge GPT Image 2 by adherence to the brief, polish, layout usefulness, and editability.
