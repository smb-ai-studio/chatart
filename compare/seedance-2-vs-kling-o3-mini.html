VIDEO MODEL COMPARISON

Seedance 2 vs Kling O3 Mini

Both routes support 4K AI video creation in ChatArt Pro. The practical difference is how you want to work: Seedance 2 is better for reference-rich, audio-aware direction; Kling O3 Mini is better for fast visual iteration.

HOW TO CHOOSE

Two strong routes, two different jobs.

Choose Seedance 2

Pick Seedance 2 when the result needs cinematic continuity, reference control, and a more deliberate audio-video plan.

Choose Kling O3 Mini

Pick Kling O3 Mini when you want to move quickly, compare visual routes, and create usable first-pass 4K clips.

TEST PROMPT

Run the same brief through both models.

For a fair comparison, use one prompt and change only the model. Example: "A cinematic 4K product reveal of [product] on a reflective surface, slow camera push, soft rim light, subtle environmental motion, final frame with clean space for headline text."Compare subject stability, camera feel, lighting, final-frame usability, and how much editing each output needs.
