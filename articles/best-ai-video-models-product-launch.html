PROMPT STARTER

Give the model a launch scene, not only a product name.

Product launch video prompt

A 4K product launch video for [product], opening with [problem or hook], showing [key feature], cinematic lighting, clean studio background, camera moves from [shot] to [shot], final frame leaves space for headline text.

COMMON MISTAKES

Avoid vague video prompts.

Do not ask only for "a product video." Include the product role, audience, desired emotion, camera movement, environment, key feature, and final frame. This makes the output easier to connect with thumbnails, captions, and ad copy.
