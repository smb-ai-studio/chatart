WHO THIS IS FOR

Creators, marketers, and growth teams building reusable campaign materials.

This playbook is useful when you have one product offer, launch message, or content hook and need videos, images, captions, ad copy, and social variants that feel connected.

WHAT YOU CAN CREATE

Ten assets from one brief.

1. Hero video concept

A short AI video that introduces the product or idea.

2. Product reveal clip

A focused scene for launch posts, ads, or landing pages.

3. Campaign key visual

A still image that anchors the look of the campaign.

4. Thumbnail or cover

A social-first image built for attention and clarity.

5. Moodboard

Visual style references for internal alignment.

6. Caption set

Short copy variations for social posts.

7. Ad copy variants

Different angles for paid creative testing.

8. Landing-page angle

A concise hook and supporting message for conversion pages.

9. Music direction

Audio mood notes for creator videos or campaign clips.

10. Channel checklist

A list of formats to adapt before publishing.

RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW

Start broad, then package the winning direction.

  1. Clarify the brief. Define the audience, promise, product proof, desired emotion, and primary channel.
  2. Create two or three visual routes. Use AI image assets for moodboards, product visuals, and thumbnails.
  3. Generate video scenes. Move the strongest direction into video for product reveals, hooks, and short clips.
  4. Write the copy around the same angle. Generate captions, ad variants, headlines, and CTA options.
  5. Assemble the asset pack. Keep the best video, image, copy, and music directions together for review or testing.

PROMPT STARTER

Use a single source brief.

Campaign brief prompt

Create a campaign asset plan for [product] targeting [audience]. The core promise is [benefit]. Generate video concepts, image directions, thumbnail ideas, captions, ad copy angles, and music mood notes for [channel].
