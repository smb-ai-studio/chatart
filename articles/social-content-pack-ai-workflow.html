SOCIAL CONTENT WORKFLOW

How creators can build a social content pack with AI.

Turn one topic or hook into the video, image, copy, and music assets needed for a complete social post package.

Build a content packSee creator use cases →

WHO THIS IS FOR

Creators and social teams producing repeatable content.

This workflow is useful when one idea needs to become several assets: a short video, a thumbnail, a caption, a music mood, and alternate hooks for different platforms.

PACK STRUCTURE

Build around one idea, not separate tools.

Short video

A primary clip or scene direction for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, or feed posts.

Cover image

A thumbnail or visual hook that makes the topic clear quickly.

Caption set

Three to five captions that test different tones and openings.

Music mood

A sound direction that supports pace, mood, and audience expectation.

Hook variants

Different first lines or visual openings for testing.

Repurpose notes

How to adapt the same asset pack across platforms.

RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW

Move from hook to publishable package.

  1. Write the core hook. State the topic, audience, and emotion in one sentence.
  2. Create the cover direction. Generate image ideas that explain the topic visually.
  3. Draft the video concept. Turn the hook into a scene, product moment, or short story.
  4. Add copy variants. Create captions, headlines, and CTAs that match the video idea.
  5. Decide the music mood. Pick the sonic direction before finalizing the edit.

PROMPT STARTER

Ask for a complete pack.

Social content pack prompt

Create a social content pack for [topic] targeting [audience]. Include a short video concept, cover image direction, three caption hooks, music mood, thumbnail text ideas, and variants for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

MODEL RECOMMENDATIONS

Use image, video, and writing routes together.

Use AI image assets for covers and thumbnails, AI video assets for short clips, and ChatArt Pro writing workflows for captions, hooks, scripts, and CTA variants.
